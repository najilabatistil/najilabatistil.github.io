ATTRACTIONS IN ROSARIO

Explore Rosario’s beautiful landscapes, landmarks, and hidden gems.

OUR LADY OF THE ROSARY PARISH

This church is a symbol of Rosario’s deep Catholic roots and devotion to Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary, after whom the town was named. The church was originally constructed in the early 20th century after the relocation of the town from its old site near Tombol Hill.

TOMBOL HILL

Tombol Hill is a significant landmark in Rosario, not only for its historical importance but also for its spiritual meaning. At the foot of the hill, visitors will find a peaceful grotto, offering a serene place for prayer and reflection. As you ascend to the top, you are greeted by a majestic statue of the Risen Christ, symbolizing hope and resurrection. The hill is surrounded by lush greenery, providing scenic views and making it a perfect spot for quiet contemplation or leisurely walks.

BATANGAS RACING CIRCUIT

Located just outside Rosario, the Batangas Racing Circuit (BRC) is a major draw for motorsport enthusiasts from all over the country. Known as one of the premier racetracks in the Philippines, it hosts a variety of car and motorcycle racing events throughout the year.

NAAMBON FALLS

Naambon Falls is a hidden gem in Rosario, perfect for nature lovers seeking tranquility. This serene waterfall, with its cool cascading waters, offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
